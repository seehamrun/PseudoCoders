Getting Started


To use Pro Planner, here's what you need to do:

  1. Log in through your Google Account on our HOME page
  2. Click the SEARCH button and fill out your preferences
  3. Review your RESULTS and add to FAVORITES if you really like a schedule!
  4. Access your FAVORITES later on -- you can even POST to our GALLERY
  5. Check out the GALLERY to see what other users are recommending!
  6. Have a GREAT day!!!
